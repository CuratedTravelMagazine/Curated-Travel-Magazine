<div class="issue-intro">


The Shades of Discovery issue explores the world through the powerful lens of color, light, and contrast. From the vivid blue corridors of the world’s most striking cities to the pristine, snow-blanketed peaks of a Chilean winter, this issue looks at how a destination's palette shapes its culture, identity, and atmosphere.
<br><br>
Through immersive storytelling, we trace the literal and historical threads that define a place—whether walking beneath the sun-dappled canopy of Funchal's Avenida Arriaga, exploring the rich, storied heritage of iconic global cocktails, or chasing the fleeting, ethereal glow of the Midnight Sun. This issue is an invitation to slow down and experience travel not just as a sequence of places, but as a rich spectrum of moods, moments, and sensory revelations.
