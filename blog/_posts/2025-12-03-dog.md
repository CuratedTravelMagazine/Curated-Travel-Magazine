At Las Ventanas al Paraíso in Mexico, the Dog Butler service pampers four-legged guests with morning beach walks, tailor-made menus, massages, and even doga classes, allowing humans to luxuriate in private pools or over-the-top spa experiences. Fireplaces in Nordic hotels, art-filled suites at the Greenwich Hotel in New York, and private rooftop pools in Santorini elevate comfort into immersive indulgence. Luxury also touches the senses in more conventional but elevated ways: over-the-top spa treatments at Canyon Ranch in Arizona with private massage suites and aromatherapy baths, helipad access in Dubai for dramatic arrivals, and temperature-controlled wine walls in Milan for personal tastings. Even the classic pillow menu at the Four Seasons Bangkok reminds us that attention to detail can be transformative, turning sleep into an experience in itself.
